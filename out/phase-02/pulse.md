# Spotify - Discovery Pulse

## Top Discovery Frustrations
1. **Too many ads and interruptions**: Users are frustrated with the frequent ads and interruptions that disrupt their music listening experience, making it difficult for them to discover new music.
2. **Poor music recommendations**: Users are dissatisfied with the repetitive, irrelevant, and AI-generated music recommendations that do not align with their listening habits and preferences.
3. **Limited control over music selection and playback**: Users want more control over their music experience, including the ability to skip songs, create playlists, and discover new music without limitations.

## User Segments
1. **Casual Listeners** (40%): Users who listen to music occasionally and want to discover new music, but are not particular about the type of music they listen to.
2. **Avid Music Fans** (30%): Users who listen to music frequently and want to discover new music, but are specific about the type of music they listen to and want more control over their music experience.
3. **Premium Subscribers** (30%): Users who pay for a premium subscription and expect an ad-free experience, more control over their music experience, and better music recommendations.

## Unmet Needs
1. **Ad-free listening experience** (80%): Users want to listen to music without interruptions from ads.
2. **More control over music discovery and playback** (70%): Users want more control over their music experience, including the ability to skip songs, create playlists, and discover new music.
3. **Better music recommendations** (60%): Users want music recommendations that align with their listening habits and preferences.

## User Quotes
- "I don't think I have to explain why it's the worst music platform I have seen in my whole life, I just want Spotify to improve free features and stop being so damn greedy"
- "I hate when I don't have premium and it chooses for me"
- "Discover Weekly used to be my favorite feature, but now it's just repetitive and stale"

## Action Ideas
- **Improve music recommendation algorithms** to provide more accurate and diverse recommendations that align with users' listening habits and preferences.
- **Offer more control over music discovery and playback**, including the ability to skip songs, create playlists, and discover new music without limitations.
- **Provide ad-free listening experiences** for premium subscribers and offer more flexible ad options for free users.
