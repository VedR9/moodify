# AI-Powered Music Discovery Engine — Spotify Growth PM Challenge

## Role & Context

**Role:** Product Manager, Growth Team at Spotify  
**Strategic Goal:** Increase meaningful music discovery and reduce repetitive listening behavior.

Spotify has successfully acquired millions of users and built one of the world's most sophisticated recommendation systems. Yet a significant percentage of listening still comes from repeat playlists, familiar artists, and previously discovered tracks — indicating that the discovery loop is broken for a meaningful user segment.

---

## Part 1 — Build an AI-Powered Review Discovery Engine

Before proposing any solution, build an AI-powered system that analyzes user feedback at scale.

### Allowed Tools / Stack
- Claude, GPTs, Agents
- Workflows, RAG systems
- n8n, Zapier, Perplexity
- Any AI-native stack of your choice

### Data Sources to Analyze
- App Store reviews
- Play Store reviews
- Reddit discussions
- Community forums
- Social media conversations

### Research Questions to Answer
1. Why do users struggle to discover new music?
2. What are the most common frustrations with recommendations?
3. What listening behaviors are users trying to achieve?
4. What causes users to repeatedly listen to the same content?
5. Which user segments experience different discovery challenges?
6. What unmet needs emerge consistently across reviews?

---

## Part 2 — Validate the Opportunity Through User Research

AI-generated insights are a starting point only. Validate findings through primary research.

- Conduct **5–6 user interviews**
- Respondents must belong to the **segment identified in Part 1**
- Goal: confirm, refine, or challenge the AI-surfaced themes

---

## Part 3 — Define the Problem

Based on combined AI + primary research, frame the problem by clearly articulating:

- **Root cause** of the discovery problem
- **Target user segment** (specific, well-defined)
- **Business case** — why solving this makes strategic sense for Spotify

---

## Part 4 — Build an AI-Native MVP

Design and build a functional MVP based on your insights.

### Format
- A prototype for a feature within the existing Spotify product, **OR**
- An agent
- Must be **deployed to production** (not just a wireframe)

### Required Explanations
- Why traditional recommendation systems are insufficient
- What AI unlocks that was previously difficult or impossible
- How AI changes the user experience
